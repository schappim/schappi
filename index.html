Marcus Schappi

About

Marcus Schappi is a serial entrepreneur who has founded and exited multiple successful technology companies. He co-founded ChipHacker.com, (acquired by Stack Exchange), as well as Ninja Blocks (went out with a ninja star).

Geek Ammo was another of his ventures, later acquired by SparkFun Electronics. He also established Little Bird Electronics and Pi Australia, both of which are still prospering. Currently, he is involved with Chick Commerce, the creators of Koi and Ninja.

Current Project

I am currently focusing on automating the world of commerce with Chick Commerce. We are building a suite of products that will help businesses of all sizes to sell more, faster, and with less effort.
